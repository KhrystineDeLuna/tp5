Ingredients

  • 2 cups superfine sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chunks

  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder for dusting (or as needed)

Directions

  1. Step 1
    • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    • Grease a 12x9-inch baking dish and dust with 1 teaspoon cocoa powder.
  2. Step 2
    • Beat eggs, superfine sugar, butter, flour, chocolate chunks, and 3/4 cups cocoa powder together with an electric hand mixer in a large mixing bowl until smooth.
    • Fold raspberries gently into the batter; spoon into the prepared baking dish.
  3. Step 3
    • Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean and the top is a bit crusty, 40 to 45 minutes.
    • Let cool completely before cutting.
