Righteous Raspberry Brownies

  • Almond Riccotta Cake
  • Banoffee Pavlova
  • Boterkoek

Kitchen Equipment

  • Chef
  • Oven
  • 12" x 9" pan
  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring spoons
  • Electric hand mixer (or stand mixer)
  • Large mixing bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 cups superfine sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chunks
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder for dusting (or as needed)

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  • Grease a 12x9-inch baking dish and dust with 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • Beat eggs, superfine sugar, butter, flour, chocolate chunks, and 3/4 cups cocoa powder together
  • With an electric hand mixer in a large mixing bowl until smooth
  • Fold raspberries gently into the batter; spoon into the prepared baking dish
  • Bake in the preheated oven 40 to 45 minutes (toothpick should be clean if inserted in center)
  • Let cool completely before cutting.
